Daily Focus

Three focuses,
every new tab.

A calm dashboard that replaces your new tab — three focuses, a Pomodoro timer, a habit grid, and an optional daily Danish phrase. Stays out of your way until you open a tab.

  • Three focuses, one frog. Pick what matters today. Mark the hardest as your frog and eat it first.
  • Pomodoro per task. Click play on any focus. A floating timer follows you across tabs.
  • Sticky pill on every page. Check off a focus without leaving the page you're on.
  • 30-day streak grid. Strict chain — any missed day breaks it. The kind that actually matters.
  • Daily Danish (optional). Spaced-repetition card with AI grading via your own Anthropic key.
  • Cloud sync across devices. Sign into Chrome with the same Google account and your data follows.
